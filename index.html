About SIX0NES

SIX0NES is a program focused on daily life skills training and job coaching for mobile individuals with different abilities. There are 6 one-hour sessions to choose as the member's starting point Monday through Friday: Home, Work, Community, Personal, Safety, and Social.

Memberships are required starting with 6 months to 1 year. Also included with the membership, SIX0NES offers certificates of completion, merit awards and celebration gatherings for members and families.

The CEO/Owner of SIX0NES has 34 consecutive years of experience working with individuals with different abilities at Orange Grove Center as a live-in house manager, medical and daily transporter, substitute job coach and holds a completed course certificate as a physical therapy aide.
